About Our Project

The Biodiversity Drone project combines cutting-edge drone technology with machine learning to monitor and protect natural ecosystems. Our drones are equipped with advanced cameras and sensors that can detect wildfires, identify poaching activities, and recognize various animal species in their natural habitats.

By leveraging artificial intelligence, we provide conservation teams with real-time data to make informed decisions and take immediate action when threats to biodiversity are detected.
